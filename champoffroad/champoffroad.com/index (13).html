Champ Off-Road Podcast Presented By Amsoil | Dillon Pointon, Tom and Ruth Schwartzburg

haley September 1, 2021 Podcast Leave a Comment

The Pro-Lite championship may be locked up, but Dillon Pointon is one to watch in Crandon. He’s garnered a trio of podiums in 2021, starting with his career first. He seems to have found his stride in the #19 Pro-Lite truck here in the 2nd half of the season. We look forward to seeing what The Big House has in store for him and the team.

Cheyne Statezny also catches up with a couple how has attended all 52 World Championships. Tom and Ruth Schwartzburg are die-hard off-road race fans and true ambassadors for Sportman racing.
