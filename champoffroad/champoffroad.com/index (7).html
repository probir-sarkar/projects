Racer Admission & Parking:

Race Teams: Only one passenger vehicle is allowed in the pit area. All other vehicles will need to park in the spectator parking lots. No exceptions.

Racer Admission & Parking Info

Spectator Parking

Upon arrival, spectators are directed to Spectator Lot 1 or 2. $5 per day parking fee. A complimentary shuttle to the admissions gate from all parking lots and camping.

Handicap parking is available in the VIP lot. A complimentary shuttle is provided to the admissions gate, track and back to the parking lot. ADA seating is accessible at the top of spectator hill.
