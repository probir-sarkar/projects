Tic Tac Toe

Start Game

  1. You play on a three by three game board
  2. The first player is known as X and the second is O. But players can alternate their position by clicking (Player One/Player Two) buttons on the top of game.
  3. Once the game started players cant alternate until finished.
  4. The active button shows which player has the next turn. For example, if the Player One button is active means the player has to play his turn.

  1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares
  2. You are X, your friend is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  3. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  4. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
